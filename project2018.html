Building A Fence for San Isidro Primary School

.

.

In 2018, volunteers from 5 different countries came together to serve on the small island of Bohol in the Philippines. The group of 10 volunteers built a fence for a small primary school in Balilihan. The school is located off of a main road where many tourists drive by, and it was fairly dangerous without a fence.

The group of 10 volunteers hosted cultural nights to allow the kids to experience other cultures outside of the island.
