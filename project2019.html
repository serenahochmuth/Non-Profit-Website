Planting Trees & Building A Fence for the Montesunting Community

.

.

In 2019, a group of 16 international volunteers set out (again) to the island of Bohol in the Philippines. The group worked at an elementary school in Montesunting in Carmen, Bohol. At this school, the volunteers planted trees and built a fence. The fence was vibrant with many colors and representation.

Most importantly, the volunteers got to spend time with the elementary school students. A huge part of these service trips is immersing into the culture, and these volunteers did just that!
